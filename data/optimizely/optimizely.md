## Overview

Optimizely is an experience optimization platform that provides recommendations, targeting, and testing without requiring a separate vector database infrastructure. It delivers personalization and experimentation at the application level.

## Features

- **A/B testing**: rigorous experimentation framework for digital experiences
- **Recommendations**: personalized content and product suggestions
- **Targeting**: rule-based and AI-driven audience segmentation
- **Full-stack experimentation**: client-side and server-side testing
- **Minimal infrastructure setup**: operates at the application layer, avoiding DB management

## Best For

Marketing and growth teams that want turnkey personalization and experimentation with minimal engineering overhead.
