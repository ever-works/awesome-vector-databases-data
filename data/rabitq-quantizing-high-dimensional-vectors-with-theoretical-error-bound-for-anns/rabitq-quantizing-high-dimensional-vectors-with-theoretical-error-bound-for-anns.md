## Overview

RaBitQ presents a quantization approach with theoretical error bounds for efficient approximate nearest neighbor search on high-dimensional vectors in Euclidean space.

## Key Contributions

- Theoretical error bounds for product quantization in Euclidean space
- Practical and asymptotically optimal quantization strategy
- Significant improvement in search accuracy with reduced memory overhead
- Published at SIGMOD 2024

## Publication

- **Venue**: SIGMOD 2024
- **Authors**: Gao et al.
- **Abbreviation**: RaBitQ

## Features

- Provably bounded quantization error
- Compatible with various search frameworks
- Memory-efficient storage of high-dimensional vectors
