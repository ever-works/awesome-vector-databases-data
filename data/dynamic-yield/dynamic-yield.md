## Overview

Dynamic Yield (a Mastercard company) is an experience optimization suite that provides recommendations, targeting, and testing without requiring teams to wire up a vector database. It solves for personalization and experimentation at the application level rather than infrastructure level.

## Features

- **Recommendations**: automated product and content recommendations
- **Targeting**: audience segmentation and personalization rules
- **Testing & experimentation**: A/B testing and multivariate testing
- **Minimal engineering**: turnkey personalization requiring minimal setup
- **Application-level optimization**: focuses on user experience rather than infra plumbing

## Best For

Marketing and growth teams that want turnkey personalization with minimal engineering overhead.
