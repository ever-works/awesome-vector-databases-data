## Overview

ParlayANN introduces a scalable and deterministic parallel framework for graph-based approximate nearest neighbor search.

## Key Contributions

- Deterministic parallel algorithms for graph-based ANNS
- Scalable to large core counts
- Maintains result quality under parallelization
- Published in PPoPP 2024
