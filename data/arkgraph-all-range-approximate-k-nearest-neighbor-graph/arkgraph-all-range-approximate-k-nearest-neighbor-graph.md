## Overview

ARKGraph constructs a versatile graph structure for all-range approximate k-nearest neighbor search, supporting flexible recall-accuracy trade-offs.

## Key Contributions

- Graph construction supporting multiple search ranges
- Flexible recall control through parameterization
- Single graph adapts to different query requirements
- Published in VLDB 2023
