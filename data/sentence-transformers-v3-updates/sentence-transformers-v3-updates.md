## Overview

Sentence Transformers v3.0 represents the largest update since the project's inception, with a completely redesigned training approach and extensive new features.

## Major Updates in v3.0

**New Training System**:
- SentenceTransformerTrainer for unified training
- Multi-GPU training support
- Enhanced loss logging
- Backward compatible with old training code

**Model Access**:
- 15,000+ pre-trained models on HuggingFace
- State-of-the-art MTEB leaderboard models
- Easy model loading and inference

## Latest Version (2026)

- Regular updates and improvements
