## Overview

HVS builds a hierarchical graph structure using Voronoi diagrams for efficient approximate nearest neighbor search.

## Key Contributions

- Voronoi diagram-based graph construction
- Hierarchical structure for multi-granularity search
- Geometric partitioning for improved pruning
- Published in VLDB 2021
