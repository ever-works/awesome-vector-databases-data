## Overview

Emergence is building a best-in-class agentic platform for automating, optimizing, and scaling enterprise workflows with precision and adaptability. The platform comprises self-improving agents built by Emergence, in addition to variant agents that the platform creates when necessary.

## Architecture

Through a plan-execute-verify framework, the platform efficiently identifies the best composition of agents that work for a given task, and iterates on these agents as needed to dynamically solve a superset of tasks in several domains such as test automation or data intelligence.

## Performance

Emergence AI achieved 86% accuracy on LongMemEval in February 2026 at a comparable latency to other systems, using RAG-like methods for agent memory.

## Company Information
