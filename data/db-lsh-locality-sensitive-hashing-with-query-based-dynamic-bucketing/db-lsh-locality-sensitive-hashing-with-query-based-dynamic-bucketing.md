## Overview

DB-LSH introduces dynamic bucketing in LSH based on query characteristics for improved retrieval accuracy.

## Key Contributions

- Query-aware dynamic bucketing strategy
- Improved accuracy over static LSH
- Extended to DB-LSH 2.0 with optimizations
- Published in ICDE 2023 and TKDE 2023

- **Authors**: Tian et al.
