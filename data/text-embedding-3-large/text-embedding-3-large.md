## Overview

text-embedding-3-large is OpenAI's most capable text embedding model, designed for high-performance semantic search and retrieval tasks.

## Features

- Supports up to 3,072 dimensions (can be reduced for storage optimization)
- High performance on English tasks with accurate results for most business needs
- Adjustable output size to save storage space while maintaining quality
- The dimensions parameter is only supported in text-embedding-3 and later models
- Superior accuracy compared to previous ada-002 model

## Pricing

- $0.13 per million tokens for text-embedding-3-large
- Flexible dimension sizing allows cost optimization
