## Overview

Qdrant Hybrid Cloud is the industry's first managed vector database that can be deployed in any environment - cloud, on-premise, or edge. It integrates Kubernetes clusters from any setting into a unified, enterprise-grade managed service.

## Key Innovation

Bring Your Own Cloud (BYOC) approach:
- Deploy Qdrant as a managed service on your infrastructure
- Maintain complete data sovereignty
- Benefit from managed service convenience
- No vendor lock-in for infrastructure
