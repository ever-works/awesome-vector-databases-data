## Overview

Prem AI is a Swiss-based platform built specifically for enterprises that need to keep AI workloads inside their own infrastructure. The company raised $19.5M and positions itself around data sovereignty with cryptographic verification for every interaction.

## Key Features

**Platform Capabilities:**
The platform covers the full model lifecycle. You upload datasets, fine-tune models, run evaluations, and deploy to production from a single interface.

**Data Sovereignty & Privacy:**
Data exists only in encrypted memory during inference. It is physically inaccessible to everyone, including Prem AI. The platform operates with a strict 'amnesiac' architecture. Your prompts and data exist only in the moment of processing. Prem AI does not train on your IP, does not log queries, and leaves no digital footprints.

**Positioning:**
Prem is an applied AI research lab dedicated to creating a future where everyone can access sovereign, private, and personalized AI.

## 2026 Developments

The Swiss jurisdiction and zero-data-retention architecture make it particularly attractive for European enterprises or any organization where data sovereignty is non-negotiable.

DeepSeek R1 proves open source can rival proprietary AI, aligning with PremAI's mission to build sovereign, censorship-resistant systems. From model distillation to agentic workflows, PremAI empowers enterprises to own, fine-tune, and scale AI securely while advancing open innovation.

## Target Market

The platform is positioned as one of several Azure OpenAI alternatives for data-sovereign enterprises in 2026, focusing on end-to-end AI lifecycle management under enterprise control.
