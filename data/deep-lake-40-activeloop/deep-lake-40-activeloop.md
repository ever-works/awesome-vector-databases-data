## Overview

Deep Lake 4.0 by Activeloop is an open-source database designed for complex, unstructured multimodal data with index-on-the-lake technology.

## Key Innovation

Index-on-the-lake enables sub-second queries from object storage with 10x cost efficiency vs in-memory databases and 2x faster performance than alternatives.

## Multimodal Support

Handles vectors, text, JSON, images, audio, video, PDFs, DICOM, and annotations in one platform.

## Pricing

Open source and Activeloop Cloud managed options available.
