# Long-Context LLMs Meet RAG

A research paper examining the intersection of long-context LLMs and Retrieval-Augmented Generation, focusing on the challenges of combining long-context windows with RAG pipelines, including the 'hard negatives' problem where irrelevant retrieved documents can degrade LLM output quality.
